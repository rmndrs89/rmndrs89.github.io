I was born on 16 December 1989 in Heesch, The Netherlands. In 2008 I completed my pre-university education (VWO atheneum) at the **Hooghuis Lyceum locatie Titus Brandsma Lyceum** in Oss. I obtained my bachelor's and master's degree in Biomedical Engineering from the **Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e)** with a focus on computational biomechanics of tissue-engineered cartilage.
In 2015 I started working for <a href="https://www.code-ps.com/">Code Product Solutions</a> where I was a *simulation engineer* for almost three years. In the beginning of 2019 I returned to academia, and started as a doctoral researcher at the **Christian-Albrechts-Universitat zu Kiel**, Germany.
