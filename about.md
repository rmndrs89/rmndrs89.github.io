I was born on 16 December 1989 in Heesch, The Netherlands. In 2008 I completed my pre-university education (VWO atheneum) at the <a href="https://tbl.hethooghuis.nl/">Hooghuis Lyceum locatie Titus Brandsma Lyceum</a> in Oss.  
I obtained my bachelor's and master's degree in Biomedical Engineering from the <a href="https://www.tue.nl/en/our-university/departments/biomedical-engineering/">Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e)</a> with a focus on computational biomechanics of tissue-engineered cartilage.  
In 2015 I started working for <a href="https://www.code-ps.com/">Code Product Solutions</a> where I was a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer-aided_engineering">simulation engineer</a> for almost three years.  
In the beginning of 2019 I returned to academia, and started as a doctoral researcher at the <a href="https://dss.tf.uni-kiel.de/">Christian-Albrechts-Universitat zu Kiel</a> and the <a href="https://neurogeriatrics-kiel.com/">Universitatsklinikum Schleswig-Holstein</a>, Germany.
